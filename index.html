Biryani

Preparation Steps:
  1. Marinate the Meat: In a bowl, mix 500g chicken or lamb with 1 cup yogurt, 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste, 1 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp red chili powder, 1 tsp garam masala, and salt. Let it marinate for 1 hour.
  2. Cook the Rice: Rinse 2 cups basmati rice and soak for 30 minutes. Boil 6 cups water with 2 bay leaves, 4 cloves, and salt. Add rice and cook until 70% done (about 5-7 minutes). Drain and set aside.
  3. Prepare the Masala: Heat 3 tbsp ghee in a pan. Add 1 sliced onion and fry until golden brown. Remove half for garnish. Add 1 tsp cumin seeds, 1 cinnamon stick, and 2 chopped tomatoes. Cook until soft. Add marinated meat and cook for 15 minutes.
  4. Layer the Biryani: In a heavy-bottomed pot, layer half the meat masala, then half the rice. Sprinkle with saffron milk (soak 10 saffron strands in 2 tbsp warm milk), fried onions, and chopped mint/coriander. Repeat layers.
  5. Dum Cooking: Cover with a tight lid and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes to let flavors meld.
  6. Serve: Gently fluff the biryani and serve hot with raita or salan.

Butter Chicken

Preparation Steps:
  1. Marinate Chicken: Mix 500g boneless chicken with 1 cup yogurt, 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste, 1 tsp red chili powder, 1 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp garam masala, salt, and 1 tbsp lemon juice. Marinate for 30 minutes or overnight.
  2. Cook Chicken: Grill or pan-fry the chicken in 2 tbsp butter until slightly charred and cooked through (10-12 minutes). Set aside.
  3. Make the Gravy: In a pan, melt 3 tbsp butter. Sauté 1 chopped onion, 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste, and 2 chopped tomatoes until soft. Add 10 cashews and cook for 5 minutes. Blend into a smooth puree.
  4. Cook the Sauce: Heat 2 tbsp butter, add the puree, 1 tsp red chili powder, 1 tsp garam masala, 1 tsp kasuri methi, and salt. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Combine: Add ½ cup heavy cream and the cooked chicken. Simmer for 5-7 minutes until flavors meld.
  6. Serve: Garnish with fresh cream and coriander. Serve with naan or rice.

Paneer Tikka

Preparation Steps:
  1. Marinate Paneer: In a bowl, mix 200g paneer cubes with ½ cup yogurt, 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste, 1 tsp red chili powder, 1 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp garam masala, 1 tbsp lemon juice, and salt. Add 1 diced bell pepper and 1 onion (cubed). Marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. Skewer: Thread paneer, bell pepper, and onion onto skewers.
  3. Grill: Preheat a grill or oven to 200°C (392°F). Grill skewers for 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until charred.
  4. Baste: Brush with melted butter midway for extra flavor.
  5. Serve: Garnish with lemon wedges and serve with mint chutney.

Masala Dosa

Preparation Steps:
  1. Prepare Batter: Soak 1 cup rice and ½ cup urad dal separately for 6 hours. Blend with water into a smooth batter. Mix, add salt, and ferment overnight.
  2. Make Filling: Boil 3 potatoes, mash them. Heat 1 tbsp oil, add 1 tsp mustard seeds, 1 tsp cumin seeds, 1 chopped onion, 2 green chilies, and curry leaves. Add mashed potatoes, ½ tsp turmeric, salt, and mix.
  3. Cook Dosa: Heat a non-stick pan, pour a ladle of batter, and spread thinly. Drizzle oil and cook until golden.
  4. Assemble: Place 2 tbsp potato filling in the center, fold the dosa, and remove from pan.
  5. Serve: Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.

Dal Makhani

Preparation Steps:
  1. Soak Lentils: Soak ¾ cup whole black urad dal and ¼ cup rajma (kidney beans) overnight.
  2. Cook Lentils: Pressure cook lentils with 4 cups water and salt for 6-8 whistles until soft. Mash slightly.
  3. Make Base: Heat 3 tbsp butter, sauté 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste, 1 chopped onion, and 2 pureed tomatoes. Add 1 tsp red chili powder, 1 tsp garam masala, and salt.
  4. Combine: Add cooked lentils, ½ cup cream, and 1 tsp kasuri methi. Simmer for 20-30 minutes on low heat.
  5. Serve: Garnish with cream and coriander. Serve with naan or rice.

Pizza

Preparation Steps:
  1. Make Dough: Mix 2 cups flour, 1 tsp yeast, 1 tsp sugar, 1 tsp salt, and ¾ cup warm water. Knead into a soft dough, cover, and let rise for 1 hour.
  2. Prepare Sauce: Cook 2 chopped tomatoes with 1 tsp garlic, 1 tsp oregano, 1 tsp basil, and salt until thick.
  3. Shape Dough: Punch down dough, roll into a 12-inch circle, and place on a pizza tray.
  4. Assemble: Spread sauce, add 1 cup mozzarella cheese, and top with sliced bell peppers, olives, and mushrooms.
  5. Bake: Bake at 220°C (425°F) for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden.
  6. Serve: Slice and serve hot.

Pasta

Preparation Steps:
  1. Boil Pasta: Cook 200g pasta in salted boiling water for 8-10 minutes until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Make Sauce: Heat 2 tbsp olive oil, sauté 1 tsp garlic, add 1 cup tomato puree, 1 tsp oregano, 1 tsp basil, and salt. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Combine: Toss pasta in sauce, add ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Serve: Garnish with fresh basil and serve hot.

Risotto

Preparation Steps:
  1. Prepare Broth: Heat 4 cups vegetable broth and keep warm.
  2. Sauté: Heat 2 tbsp butter, sauté 1 chopped onion and 1 tsp garlic until soft. Add 1 cup Arborio rice and toast for 2 minutes.
  3. Cook Rice: Add ½ cup white wine, stir until absorbed. Add broth ½ cup at a time, stirring until absorbed, for 20-25 minutes until creamy.
  4. Finish: Stir in ½ cup Parmesan cheese and 1 tbsp butter.
  5. Serve: Garnish with parsley and serve immediately.

Tiramisu

Preparation Steps:
  1. Prepare Cream: Beat 1 cup heavy cream until stiff peaks form. In another bowl, mix 250g mascarpone cheese, ½ cup sugar, and 1 tsp vanilla extract. Fold in whipped cream.
  2. Coffee Mix: Brew 1 cup strong coffee, add 2 tbsp coffee liqueur, and cool.
  3. Assemble: Dip 200g ladyfingers in coffee mix briefly, layer in a dish. Spread half the cream mixture over them. Repeat layers.
  4. Chill: Refrigerate for 4 hours.
  5. Serve: Dust with cocoa powder before serving.

Lasagna

Preparation Steps:
  1. Make Meat Sauce: Cook 500g ground beef with 1 chopped onion and 2 tsp garlic. Add 2 cups tomato sauce, 1 tsp oregano, 1 tsp basil, and salt. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  2. Prepare Cheese Mix: Mix 1 cup ricotta, 1 egg, and ½ cup Parmesan cheese.
  3. Assemble: In a baking dish, layer cooked lasagna noodles, meat sauce, cheese mix, and 1 cup mozzarella. Repeat for 3 layers.
  4. Bake: Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 40 minutes until bubbly.
  5. Serve: Let rest for 10 minutes, then slice and serve.

Borscht

Preparation Steps:
  1. Prepare Beets: Boil 3 beets until tender (30-40 minutes), peel, and grate.
  2. Cook Base: Heat 2 tbsp oil, sauté 1 chopped onion, 1 carrot (grated), and 1 tsp garlic. Add grated beets and 2 chopped potatoes.
  3. Simmer: Add 4 cups vegetable broth, 1 cup shredded cabbage, 1 tbsp vinegar, and salt. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. Finish: Stir in 1 tsp dill and adjust seasoning.
  5. Serve: Serve hot with a dollop of sour cream.

Beef Stroganoff

Preparation Steps:
  1. Cook Beef: Slice 500g beef into strips. Heat 2 tbsp butter, brown beef, and set aside.
  2. Sauté: In the same pan, sauté 1 chopped onion and 1 cup sliced mushrooms until soft.
  3. Make Sauce: Add 1 tbsp flour, stir, then add 1 cup beef broth and 1 tsp mustard. Simmer until thickened.
  4. Combine: Return beef to pan, add ½ cup sour cream, and heat through.
  5. Serve: Serve over egg noodles or rice, garnished with parsley.

Pelmeni

Preparation Steps:
  1. Make Dough: Mix 2 cups flour, 1 egg, ½ cup water, and salt. Knead until smooth, rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare Filling: Mix 300g ground beef/pork, 1 chopped onion, 1 tsp garlic, salt, and pepper.
  3. Shape: Roll dough thin, cut into 3-inch circles, place 1 tsp filling in center, fold, and seal edges.
  4. Cook: Boil in salted water for 5-7 minutes until they float.
  5. Serve: Serve with sour cream or butter.

Olivier Salad

Preparation Steps:
  1. Cook Vegetables: Boil 3 potatoes, 2 carrots, and 2 eggs until tender. Cool, peel, and dice.
  2. Combine: Mix diced vegetables with 1 cup peas, 4 chopped pickles, and 1 diced onion.
  3. Dress: Add ½ cup mayonnaise, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  4. Chill: Refrigerate for 1 hour.
  5. Serve: Garnish with dill and serve cold.

Blini

Preparation Steps:
  1. Make Batter: Mix 1 cup flour, 1 cup milk, 1 egg, 1 tsp sugar, ½ tsp salt, and 1 tsp yeast. Rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Cook: Heat a non-stick pan with butter, pour small amounts of batter, and cook until golden on both sides (1-2 minutes per side).
  3. Serve: Serve with sour cream, jam, or honey.

Fried Rice

Preparation Steps:
  1. Cook Rice: Cook 2 cups rice and cool completely (preferably day-old rice).
  2. Sauté: Heat 2 tbsp oil in a wok, scramble 2 eggs, and set aside. Add 1 chopped onion, ½ cup diced carrots, and ½ cup peas. Stir-fry for 3 minutes.
  3. Combine: Add rice, 3 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tsp sesame oil, and eggs. Stir-fry for 5 minutes.
  4. Serve: Garnish with green onions and serve hot.

Dumplings

Preparation Steps:
  1. Make Dough: Mix 2 cups flour with ¾ cup water. Knead until smooth, rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare Filling: Mix 1 cup shredded cabbage, ½ cup chopped mushrooms, 1 tsp ginger, 1 tbsp soy sauce, and salt.
  3. Shape: Roll dough into a log, cut into 20 pieces, roll each into a circle, add 1 tsp filling, and fold into crescents.
  4. Cook: Steam for 10 minutes or pan-fry until golden.
  5. Serve: Serve with soy dipping sauce.

Kung Pao Chicken

Preparation Steps:
  1. Marinate Chicken: Mix 500g diced chicken with 2 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp cornstarch, and 1 tsp sesame oil. Rest for 20 minutes.
  2. Prepare Sauce: Mix 2 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp vinegar, 1 tbsp sugar, and 1 tsp cornstarch.
  3. Stir-Fry: Heat 2 tbsp oil, fry ¼ cup peanuts and 4 dried chilies, set aside. Stir-fry chicken until cooked, add 1 diced bell pepper and 2 green onions.
  4. Combine: Add sauce and peanuts, stir until thickened.
  5. Serve: Serve with rice.

Spring Rolls

Preparation Steps:
  1. Prepare Filling: Stir-fry 1 cup shredded cabbage, ½ cup carrots, ½ cup bean sprouts, 1 tsp ginger, and 1 tbsp soy sauce. Cool.
  2. Assemble: Place 1 tbsp filling on a spring roll wrapper, fold sides, and roll tightly.
  3. Fry: Heat oil to 180°C (350°F), fry rolls until golden (2-3 minutes).
  4. Serve: Serve with sweet chili sauce.

Hot & Sour Soup

Preparation Steps:
  1. Prepare Broth: Bring 4 cups vegetable broth to a boil.
  2. Add Ingredients: Add ½ cup sliced mushrooms, ¼ cup bamboo shoots, 1 tsp ginger, and 1 tbsp soy sauce. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Thicken: Mix 1 tbsp cornstarch with 2 tbsp water, add to soup with 2 tbsp vinegar and 1 tsp white pepper.
  4. Finish: Stir in 1 beaten egg slowly, add 1 tsp sesame oil.
  5. Serve: Garnish with green onions and serve hot.

Sushi

Preparation Steps:
  1. Cook Rice: Cook 1 cup sushi rice with 1¼ cups water. Mix with 2 tbsp rice vinegar, 1 tbsp sugar, and 1 tsp salt. Cool.
  2. Prepare Filling: Slice 100g raw salmon and 1 avocado into thin strips.
  3. Roll: Place a nori sheet on a bamboo mat, spread rice, add salmon and avocado, roll tightly, and seal with water.
  4. Slice: Cut into 6-8 pieces with a wet knife.
  5. Serve: Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.

Tacos

Preparation Steps:
  1. Cook Meat: Cook 500g ground beef with 1 chopped onion, 1 tsp garlic, 1 tsp chili powder, 1 tsp cumin, and salt.
  2. Prepare Toppings: Chop 1 tomato, 1 cup lettuce, and grate ½ cup cheese.
  3. Warm Tortillas: Heat 8 tortillas in a pan or oven.
  4. Assemble: Fill tortillas with meat, top with salsa, cheese, and lettuce.
  5. Serve: Serve with lime wedges.

Shawarma

Preparation Steps:
  1. Marinate Chicken: Mix 500g chicken with ½ cup yogurt, 2 tsp garlic, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp paprika, 1 tsp cinnamon, and salt. Marinate for 1 hour.
  2. Cook: Grill or pan-fry chicken until cooked (10-12 minutes).
  3. Prepare Sauce: Mix ½ cup tahini, 2 tbsp lemon juice, 1 tsp garlic, and water.
  4. Assemble: Slice chicken, wrap in pita with lettuce, tomato, and tahini sauce.
  5. Serve: Serve warm.

Paella

Preparation Steps:
  1. Sauté: Heat 3 tbsp olive oil, cook 500g chicken and 200g shrimp until browned. Set aside.
  2. Cook Base: Sauté 1 chopped onion, 1 bell pepper, and 2 tsp garlic. Add 1 cup chopped tomatoes and 1 tsp saffron.
  3. Add Rice: Stir in 1½ cups short-grain rice and 3 cups chicken broth. Add chicken and shrimp back.
  4. Simmer: Cook uncovered for 20-25 minutes until rice is tender.
  5. Serve: Garnish with parsley and lemon wedges.

Poutine

Preparation Steps:
  1. Fry Potatoes: Cut 4 potatoes into fries, fry in oil at 180°C (350°F) until golden.
  2. Make Gravy: Melt 2 tbsp butter, stir in 2 tbsp flour, add 2 cups beef broth, and simmer until thickened.
  3. Assemble: Place fries in a dish, top with 1 cup cheese curds, and pour hot gravy over.
  4. Serve: Serve immediately while hot.

Moussaka

Preparation Steps:
  1. Prepare Eggplant: Slice 2 eggplants, salt, and rest for 30 minutes. Rinse, pat dry, and fry until golden.
  2. Cook Meat: Cook 500g ground lamb with 1 chopped onion, 2 tsp garlic, 1 cup tomato sauce, 1 tsp cinnamon, and salt.
  3. Make Béchamel: Melt 3 tbsp butter, stir in 3 tbsp flour, add 2 cups milk, and cook until thick. Add 1 egg yolk and ½ cup Parmesan.
  4. Assemble: Layer eggplant, meat sauce, and béchamel in a dish.
  5. Bake: Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 45 minutes until golden.
  6. Serve: Let rest for 10 minutes, then serve.
